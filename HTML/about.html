About Lounge Seminar

Lounge Seminar (est. 2022) is a student-led initiative aimed at fostering the exchange of ideas and knowledge between students, alumni, and faculty.

We organize frequent meetings - open to all students and interested persons - where eager students (undergraduate/graduate) make presentations on topics they're interested in and would like to share with their peers. Other invited guest speakers consist of SAMPS Professors and alumni who can showcase their own research topics, life path and choices that led them throughout their career.

Our shared goal is to provide a welcoming environment where students can grow, connect with experts in their fields of interest, engage with peers, and interact with academic and research staff in Mathematics and Physics — all within a relaxed and friendly atmosphere (with snacks, of course, for the loungeness!).

(Student) Presentations

At Lounge Seminar, we provide a safe space for students to present their ideas, past work, passion projects and fields of interest.

Your presentation can be anything from classwork to an interesting book you read related to our studies. As long as it fits the criterion of academic passion, we are here to give you space to stand on stage, practice your public speaking and share your thoughts with others.

Ask Me Anything

Have you ever wondered: who are your professors? What do they do? What is their field of expertise? How did they get to where they are now? What choices did they make in life? How can I follow their path? With whom should I collaborate on my thesis?

These answers and more are provided to you through our Ask Me Anything sessions, where the invited professors answer anything you want to ask them - as long as it remains respectful!

Other events

On occasion, we bring to you other interesting events, like book presentations, alumni talks and collaborations with other organizations/clubs.

At Lounge Seminar, we firmly believe in grasping every opportunity! Whether that means reading about historical figures that shaped the sciences we study today, networking with past NTUA generations or bringing light to other student activities, we are your portal to continuous development and self-discovery!
